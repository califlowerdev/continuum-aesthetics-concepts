Questions

Financing, answered

Does applying affect my credit score?

No. Cherry uses a soft credit check to show your options, and a soft check does not affect your score. There is no hard credit check to apply.

What can I finance?

Treatment plans across the menu, from injectables to wellness programs like peptides, GLP-1 weight management and BHRT. Ask us at your consultation and we will structure the plan with financing in mind.

How much can I be approved for?

Cherry approves qualifying patients for amounts up to $50,000. Your specific amount and terms are shown the moment you apply.

When do I apply, before or after booking?

Either works. Many patients apply before their consultation so the numbers conversation is already settled. The application link works any time, from any device.
