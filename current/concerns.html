Nobody wakes up wanting a syringe of anything. You notice something in the mirror, or in how you feel, and you want it handled well. These six starting points map your concern to the right physician-led plan.

01 · Concern

Lines & Wrinkles

Expression lines around the eyes, forehead lines that show up in photos, the "elevens" between the brows. They deepen slowly, then suddenly. The fix is not freezing your face. It is relaxing the right muscles by the right amount, so you look rested instead of treated.

Dr. Brunson maps each injection point before treating and doses conservatively by default. The most repeated line in our reviews says it best: people cannot tell what was done, only that you look great.

02 · Concern

Volume & Contour

Cheeks that have flattened, lips that have thinned, a jawline that lost its line. Volume loss is structural, so the answer is structural too: restoring support where the face has stopped producing it.

Hyaluronic acid fillers give immediate, reversible refinement. Biostimulators like Sculptra and Radiesse rebuild your own collagen over months. Most plans blend both, and every plan is designed around your anatomy at consultation.

What helps

HA Fillers Sculptra Radiesse PRF

03 · Concern

Skin Quality

Texture that makeup will not sit on, enlarged pores, acne scarring, dullness, hollow under-eyes. These respond best to regenerative work: treatments that make your skin rebuild itself rather than covering the problem.

Microneedling triggers controlled renewal, exosomes accelerate it, and PRF puts your own growth factors exactly where they are needed. Gradual, natural, and chemical-free where possible.

What helps

Microneedling Exosomes PRF Sculptra

04 · Concern

Hair Thinning

A widening part, more scalp at the temples, a ponytail that feels lighter. Hair thinning rewards early action, because dormant follicles are easier to wake than gone ones.

PRF hair restoration concentrates your own platelets and returns them to the scalp, stimulating follicles and circulation. It is a planned series, not a one-off, and your schedule is set at consultation.

GLP-1 therapies help regulate appetite and support metabolic function under genuine medical supervision: a physician consultation first, a personalized plan, and follow-up that keeps progress honest.
