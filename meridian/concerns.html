Concerns We Treat

Start with what you want to improve.

Patients rarely arrive asking for a product. They arrive with a concern. Browse the six we treat most, then see which treatments belong in the conversation.

Concern 01

Lines & wrinkles

Botulinum toxin Microneedling Exosomes

Expression lines around the eyes, forehead, and brows are usually the first thing patients ask about. The goal here is never a frozen face. It is a rested, subtly lifted look that still moves like you. Botulinum toxin softens the muscles behind the lines, while collagen-building treatments improve the skin the lines live in.

Discuss this at a consultation →

Concern 02

Volume loss

HA fillers Sculptra Radiesse PRF

Cheeks flatten, temples hollow, and lips lose definition as collagen production slows. Restoring volume well is an anatomy problem first and a product choice second. Depending on your goals, the plan may use hyaluronic acid for immediate definition, biostimulators for gradual structural support, or PRF for delicate areas like under the eyes.

Discuss this at a consultation →

Concern 03

Skin quality

Microneedling Exosomes PRF

Texture, pores, scarring, dullness. When the surface of the skin is the concern, regenerative treatments that build collagen tend to beat quick fixes. Microneedling triggers your skin’s own renewal response, and pairing it with exosomes or PRF supports healing and glow. Chemical peels are also available by consultation.

Discuss this at a consultation →

Concern 04

Hair thinning

PRF hair restoration

Thinning hair rarely announces itself all at once, which is why earlier conversations tend to mean more options. PRF hair restoration uses your own platelets to stimulate dormant follicles, improve scalp circulation, and support long-term growth, starting at the root.

Discuss this at a consultation →

Concern 05

Weight & metabolism

GLP-1 therapies Peptides

Sustainable weight management is medicine, not willpower. GLP-1 therapies help regulate appetite, reduce cravings, and support metabolic function, and peptide protocols can support recovery and lean body composition. Plans are personalized to your goals and managed by a physician from the first consultation on.

Discuss this at a consultation →

Concern 06

Hormones & energy

BHRT Peptides

Energy, sleep, mood, libido, and body composition often shift together when hormones change. BHRT plans are built from your symptoms, health history, and lab results, with ongoing monitoring rather than a set-and-forget prescription. Dedicated hormone consultations are available for men and women.
