The Continuum Method

Assess. Plan. Treat. Maintain.

Four steps, applied to every patient, every time. This is what physician-led means in practice, not as a tagline.

Why a Method

Good results are planned, not improvised.

Dr. Brunson came to aesthetics from emergency medicine, a specialty where structure is what keeps people safe. The Continuum Method brings that same discipline to aesthetic and wellness care: a defined sequence that puts assessment before product, and your long-term look before any single appointment.

It is also why the practice runs by appointment only. Consultations are unhurried, treatments are never squeezed in, and you will not be pushed into more than you need.

The Four Steps

How your plan comes together.

Step one

Assess

Your first visit is a consultation, not a treatment. Dr. Brunson reviews your goals, your health history, and your facial anatomy, and for wellness programs like peptides and BHRT, your lab results. The point is to understand what is actually driving the concern before deciding how to treat it.

Step two

Plan

Together you set a plan with clear priorities and honest timelines. Some treatments give immediate results, others build progressively over months, and the plan says which is which up front. Financing through Cherry can be arranged at this stage so the plan fits your budget as well as your goals.

Step three

Treat

Treatments are performed under physician supervision with a deliberately conservative hand. The practice philosophy is subtle enhancement: refining your features while keeping your look unmistakably you. Patients consistently describe the approach as precise, methodical, and unrushed.

Step four

Maintain

Results are reviewed and maintained over time rather than left to fade. Wellness programs include ongoing monitoring, and aesthetic plans evolve as you do. If you have questions between visits, you can reach the practice directly by text or WhatsApp.

The Continuum Standard

What you can expect, always.

Consultation first

No treatment happens before an in-person consultation. Candidacy, technique, and dosing are decided with you, not for you.

Physician supervision

Continuum is led by Dr. Adam Brunson, MD, board certified in emergency medicine. All care is performed under physician supervision.

No pressure, ever

Reviews mention it again and again: nothing is oversold and nothing is rushed. If less is the right answer, that is the recommendation you will get.

Direct access
