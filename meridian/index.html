Good to Know

Questions, answered.

Is Continuum Aesthetics medically supervised?

Yes. Continuum Aesthetics is a physician-led practice. Dr. Adam Brunson, MD plans every treatment, and all care is performed under physician supervision following an in-person consultation.

Will my results look natural?

Natural is the entire point. The practice philosophy is conservative by design: subtle enhancement that refines your features while keeping your look unmistakably you. Individual results may vary, which is one more reason every plan starts with a consultation.

Where are you located, and is parking easy?

We are in Brickell at 175 SW 7th Street, Suite 1411, Miami, FL 33130. Valet parking is complimentary, so arriving is the easiest part of your visit.

Do you offer financing?

Yes. We partner with Cherry, which offers 0% APR options for qualified applicants and plans up to $50,000. Checking your options does not require a hard credit check. See the financing page for details.
