Territory 01

Face & Contour

Expression lines, lost volume, lips and overall facial balance, treated with a conservative hand and a clear plan.

Injectables

Botulinum Toxin

"Botox" injections are a refined, minimally invasive treatment that smooths expression lines and refreshes your natural look. By relaxing targeted facial muscles, they soften fine lines around the eyes, forehead and brows, without compromising your ability to express yourself. The physician's goal is a rested, subtly lifted appearance that enhances your features without looking overdone.

  • Softens lines around the eyes, forehead and brows
  • Keeps your natural expression intact
  • Performed by a physician, conservative dosing
Injectables

Hyaluronic Acid Fillers

Hyaluronic acid fillers restore volume, enhance facial contours and hydrate the skin from within for a naturally youthful appearance. Whether defining lips, lifting cheeks or smoothing lines, these versatile treatments offer immediate, elegant results with minimal downtime. The goal is subtle enhancement that refines your features while keeping your look unmistakably you.

  • Immediate, elegant definition
  • Minimal downtime
  • Lips, cheeks, contours and lines
Biostimulators

Sculptra

Sculptra is a biostimulatory injectable that gradually restores facial volume by stimulating your body's own collagen production. Rather than creating an immediate filled look, it works over time to improve skin structure, firmness and overall facial support. It is especially suited to patients seeking subtle rejuvenation with results that build progressively, customized to your anatomy and desired level of correction.

  • Stimulates your own collagen
  • Results build progressively
  • Tailored to your anatomy and goals
Biostimulators

Radiesse

Radiesse is a biostimulatory filler that provides immediate volume and contour while also stimulating collagen and elastin production over time. It can enhance facial structure, improve skin quality and restore support in areas affected by volume loss or laxity. It is often chosen by patients who want both visible correction and longer-term regenerative benefit, tailored to create balanced, natural-looking results.

  • Immediate volume and contour
  • Collagen and elastin support over time
  • Balanced, natural-looking correction

Territory 02

Skin & Regenerative

Treatments that work with your own biology to improve texture, tone and glow, gradually and without harsh interventions.

Regenerative

PRF, Platelet Rich Fibrin

PRF treatments use your body's own regenerative power to rejuvenate skin, stimulate collagen and restore a healthy glow. Rich in growth factors, PRF is gently injected or applied topically to improve texture, under-eye hollows and overall tone, naturally and gradually. It is an elegant, chemical-free way to refresh the skin from the inside out.

  • Uses your body's own growth factors
  • Improves texture, under-eye hollows and tone
  • Natural, gradual, chemical-free
Regenerative

Exosomes

Exosome treatments harness regenerative science to repair, renew and revitalize the skin at a cellular level. Rich in signaling molecules, exosomes support collagen production, improve texture and enhance radiance. Ideal after microneedling, they accelerate healing and elevate your glow, subtly and powerfully.

  • Cell-level repair and renewal
  • Supports collagen and radiance
  • Ideal paired with microneedling
Regenerative

Microneedling

Microneedling is a sophisticated treatment that stimulates collagen and elastin by creating tiny, controlled micro-injuries. This natural healing response smooths fine lines, refines texture and reduces the appearance of pores and scars. The result is firmer, fresher skin with a luminous finish, renewed without harsh interventions.

  • Stimulates collagen and elastin
  • Refines texture, pores and scars
  • Firmer, fresher, luminous skin
Resurfacing

Chemical Peels

Chemical peels resurface the skin to improve tone, clarity and texture. Performed by consultation, the depth and formulation are matched to your skin and your goals, so the treatment is appropriate rather than aggressive. Often planned alongside regenerative treatments as part of a longer skin-quality program.

  • Improves tone, clarity and texture
  • Depth matched to your skin by consultation
  • Pairs well with a skin-quality program

Territory 03

Body & Wellness

Physician-managed programs for weight, metabolism, energy and hormones. This is where having an MD in the room matters most.

Weight Management

GLP-1 Weight Management

GLP-1 therapies support healthy weight management by helping regulate appetite, reduce cravings and promote a greater sense of fullness. They can also support metabolic function and more stable blood sugar. Combined with healthy habits, they can encourage meaningful, long-term progress. Plans are personalized to each patient's goals, with a focus on safety and ongoing support.

  • Helps regulate appetite and cravings
  • Supports metabolic function
  • Personalized and physician-supervised
Longevity

Peptides

Peptide therapies support the body's natural signaling pathways involved in repair, recovery, metabolism, skin health and overall vitality. Depending on the peptide selected, they may help promote lean body composition, improve exercise recovery, support collagen production and encourage tissue repair. Plans are customized to your goals, lab findings and health profile, with a focus on long-term optimization.

  • Supports repair, recovery and metabolism
  • Customized to goals and lab findings
  • Physician-managed, long-term optimization
Hormone Health

Bioidentical Hormone Replacement Therapy

BHRT helps restore hormonal balance using hormones that are structurally similar to those the body produces naturally. When hormone changes are contributing to symptoms, treatment may support improvements in energy, mood, sleep, libido, body composition and overall wellness. Plans are personalized to your goals, history, symptoms and lab results, with ongoing monitoring rather than a set-and-forget prescription. Dedicated male hormone consultations are available.

  • May support energy, mood, sleep and libido
  • Built from symptoms and lab results
  • Ongoing physician monitoring

Territory 04

Hair Restoration

A natural, science-backed approach to thinning hair, using your own platelets.

Restoration

PRF Hair Restoration

Hair loss treatments with PRF offer a natural, science-backed approach to fuller, healthier hair. Using your own platelets, PRF stimulates dormant follicles, improves scalp circulation and supports long-term hair growth. It is a subtle yet powerful solution for thinning hair, designed to revive your hair from the root. Earlier conversations tend to mean more options, so it is worth raising sooner rather than later.

  • Uses your own platelets
  • Stimulates dormant follicles
  • Supports long-term hair growth

Also available by consultation: filler dissolving, SELPHYL under-eye PRF, P-Tox and male hormone consultations. Mention any of these when you book and we will set aside the right amount of time.
