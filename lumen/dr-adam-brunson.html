The story

From the ER
to aesthetics

Dr. Brunson trained where the stakes are highest. After earning his MD at the University of South Carolina School of Medicine, he completed his emergency medicine residency at Jackson Memorial Hospital with the University of Miami, one of the busiest emergency programs in the country. He remains on active staff at South Miami Hospital, part of Baptist Health.

Emergency medicine teaches a specific kind of discipline: assess thoroughly, act precisely and never treat the chart instead of the person. That training is all over the way Continuum runs. Injections are mapped before they happen. Plans are explained in plain language. And nothing is recommended that the patient does not actually need.

He founded Continuum Aesthetics in 2021 and opened the practice's dedicated Brickell suite in 2025, building it around a simple idea: aesthetic and wellness medicine deserves the same rigor as any other kind. That is why the menu spans both, from botulinum toxin and fillers to GLP-1 weight management, peptides and hormone therapy, all under one physician's supervision.

Patients notice. The practice holds a 5.0 rating across 30 Google reviews, and the most repeated themes are the ones that matter: natural results, methodical technique and time taken with every visit.
