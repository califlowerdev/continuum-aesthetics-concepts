Why an ER doctor?

Emergency medicine is the specialty of staying calm, reading anatomy fast, and doing exactly what is needed and nothing more. It is also the training you want in the rare moment something does not go to plan.

At Continuum Aesthetics, that means physician-level screening before treatment, physician supervision during it, and a physician you can actually reach afterward.

The Continuum Method

One method, every treatment

Whether you come in for tox or a full longevity plan, the path is the same. It is the reason results here stay subtle, and the reason patients stay for years.

01

Assess

A one-on-one consultation. Your history, your features, your goals, and where labs or imaging add clarity. Nothing is booked before this.

02

Plan

A written plan that maps what to do, what to skip, and in what order. If the right answer is "not yet" or "not you," that is the plan.

03

Treat

Conservative, precise treatment performed under physician supervision, with screening and safety protocols carried over from the ER.

04

Maintain

Scheduled follow-ups and small adjustments over time. Good aesthetic work is a relationship, not a transaction.
