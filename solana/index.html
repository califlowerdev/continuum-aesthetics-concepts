The Continuum Method

Assess. Plan. Treat. Maintain.

No menu roulette and no upsell. Every patient begins with a physician consultation, leaves with a plan built around their anatomy and goals, and is followed over time, because good work should age as well as you do.

01

Assess

A one-on-one consultation with Dr. Brunson. Your history, your features, your goals, and where labs or imaging can add clarity.

02

Plan

A clear, personalized plan that maps what to do, what to skip, and in what order. You will never be talked into more than you need.

03

Treat

Precise, conservative treatment performed under physician supervision, with safety protocols that come from emergency medicine.

04

Maintain

Follow-up and fine-tuning across the year, so results stay subtle and natural through every chapter.
