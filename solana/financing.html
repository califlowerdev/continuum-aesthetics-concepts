Questions

Financing, answered

Will applying hurt my credit? +

No hard credit check is required to apply, so checking your options will not leave a hard inquiry on your credit report.

What can financing cover? +

Cherry plans can apply to treatment at the practice, from a single visit to the full plan you build with Dr. Brunson, up to $50,000 for qualifying patients.

Do I have to pay interest? +

Qualifying patients can access 0% APR options. Exact terms depend on your application and the plan you choose, and Cherry shows them to you clearly before you commit.

When should I apply? +

Any time. Many patients apply right after their consultation, once the plan and total are clear. You can also apply before you ever visit, just to understand your options. Ask about financing during your consultation and we will walk you through it.
