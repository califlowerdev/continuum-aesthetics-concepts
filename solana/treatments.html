Injectables & Biostimulators

Smooth expression lines and restore facial support with conservative, anatomy-first dosing. The goal is a rested, subtly lifted look that keeps your expression intact.

Smooth · Soften · Refresh

Botulinum Toxin

"Botox" injections are a refined, minimally invasive treatment designed to smooth expression lines and refresh your natural look. By relaxing targeted facial muscles, they soften fine lines around the eyes, forehead, and brows, without compromising your ability to express yourself. The result is a rested, subtly lifted appearance that enhances your features without looking overdone. Perfect for those seeking a polished, effortless glow.

Gradual · Collagen · Structure

Sculptra

Sculptra is a biostimulatory injectable designed to gradually restore facial volume by stimulating your body's natural collagen production. Rather than creating an immediate "filled" look, Sculptra works over time to improve skin structure, firmness, and overall facial support. It is especially helpful for patients seeking subtle, natural-looking rejuvenation with results that build progressively. Treatment plans are customized based on your anatomy, goals, and desired level of correction.

Radiesse

Radiesse is a biostimulatory filler that provides immediate volume and contour while also stimulating collagen and elastin production over time. Radiesse is often chosen for patients who want both visible correction and longer-term regenerative benefits. Regenerative Skin

Treatments that put your own biology to work: platelets, signaling molecules and controlled collagen stimulation for texture, tone and glow.

Natural · Chemical-free · Gradual

PRF (Platelet Rich Fibrin)

PRF treatments use your body's own regenerative power to rejuvenate skin, stimulate collagen, and restore a healthy glow. Packed with growth factors, PRF is gently injected or applied topically to improve texture, under-eye hollows, and overall tone, naturally and gradually. It's an elegant, chemical-free way to refresh your skin from the inside out.

Wellness & Longevity

Looking your best starts below the surface. Physician-monitored programs built from your labs, your symptoms and your goals.

Recovery · Vitality · Optimization

Peptides

Peptide therapies are designed to support the body's natural signaling pathways involved in repair, recovery, metabolism, skin health, and overall vitality. Depending on the peptide selected, they may help promote lean body composition, improve exercise recovery, support collagen production, encourage tissue repair, and optimize wellness from the inside out. These therapies are often customized based on individual goals, lab findings, and overall health profile. At Continuum Aesthetics, peptide treatment plans are approached thoughtfully, with a focus on personalized care, safety, and long-term optimization.

Appetite · Metabolism · Sustainability

Weight Loss / GLP-1s

GLP-1 therapies are designed to support healthy weight management by helping regulate appetite, reduce cravings, and promote a greater sense of fullness. They can also support improved metabolic function and more stable blood sugar regulation, making them a valuable tool for many patients seeking sustainable results. When combined with healthy lifestyle habits, these treatments may help encourage meaningful, long-term progress in weight and overall wellness. At Continuum Aesthetics, GLP-based treatment plans are personalized to each patient's goals, with a focus on safety, support, and lasting success.

Balance · Energy · Monitoring

Bioidentical Hormone Replacement Therapy (BHRT)

Bioidentical hormone replacement therapy, or BHRT, is designed to help restore hormonal balance using hormones that are structurally similar to those naturally produced by the body. Treatment may support improvements in energy, mood, sleep, libido, body composition, and overall wellness when hormone changes are contributing to symptoms. Plans are personalized based on each patient's goals, health history, symptoms, and lab results. At Continuum Aesthetics, BHRT is approached thoughtfully with ongoing monitoring to support safe, balanced, and optimized care.
