Your Needs

Start with what you feel, not what to ask for

You do not need to arrive knowing the difference between a biostimulator and a peptide. Tell us what you have noticed, and the consultation does the translating. Below, the six concerns we hear most, and where each conversation usually begins.

  • Lines & Wrinkles
  • Volume Loss
  • Skin Quality
  • Hair Thinning
  • Weight & Metabolism
  • Hormones & Energy
01 · What you might be noticing

Lines & wrinkles

Expression lines around the eyes, forehead and brows that linger after the expression has passed. Fine lines that makeup has started settling into. You still want to look like you, just the rested version.

How we approach it: conservative dosing mapped to your anatomy, so movement stays natural. Many patients combine muscle-relaxing treatment with collagen-stimulating skin work over time.

Where the conversation usually starts
Botulinum Toxin HA Fillers Microneedling Exosomes

02 · What you might be noticing

Volume loss & contour

Cheeks that read flatter than they used to, hollowing under the eyes, lips that have lost definition, or a jawline that feels less supported. Often it is not one dramatic change, just a slow shift in how light sits on your face.

How we approach it: structure first. Dr. Brunson maps where support has changed and chooses between immediate restoration and gradual collagen building, or a sequenced mix of both.

Where the conversation usually starts
HA Fillers Sculptra Radiesse PRF for under-eyes
Book a Consultation →
03 · What you might be noticing

Skin quality & texture

Dullness, rough texture, visible pores, old acne scarring, or skin that has simply lost its glow. Nothing is wrong exactly, but the surface no longer reflects how you feel.

How we approach it: regenerative treatments that work with your own biology, stimulating collagen and elastin gradually rather than resurfacing aggressively.

Where the conversation usually starts
Microneedling Exosomes PRF Chemical peels, in consultation
Book a Consultation →
04 · What you might be noticing

Hair thinning

A widening part, more hair in the brush, a hairline that photographs differently than it used to. Thinning tends to arrive quietly, which is exactly why early, consistent treatment matters.

How we approach it: PRF made from your own platelets, injected where follicles have gone quiet, with a realistic timeline discussed up front. Growth is gradual and is monitored across visits.

Where the conversation usually starts
PRF Hair Restoration
Book a Consultation →
05 · What you might be noticing

Weight & metabolism

The habits that used to work are not working. Cravings drive the day more than they should, energy dips mid-afternoon, and the scale will not move no matter how disciplined the week was.

How we approach it: medically, not aspirationally. A physician consultation and labs come first, then a personalized plan that may pair GLP-1 therapy or peptides with lifestyle support, monitored over time.

Where the conversation usually starts
Weight Loss / GLP-1s Peptides BHRT
Book a Consultation →
06 · What you might be noticing

Hormones & energy

Sleep that does not restore, mood that drifts, libido and drive that have faded, or a body composition shift you cannot train your way out of. When hormones change, everything downstream changes with them.

How we approach it: labs and history before anything else. If hormone changes are contributing to your symptoms, BHRT is personalized and monitored on an ongoing basis. Dedicated male hormone consultations are available.

Where the conversation usually starts
BHRT Peptides Male hormone consultation
