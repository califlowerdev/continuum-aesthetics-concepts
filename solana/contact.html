Post-Treatment Support

Care does not end when the appointment does

Continuum Aesthetics is a physician-led practice, and that matters most after you leave. If you are an existing patient with a question or concern following a treatment, reach us directly on WhatsApp or by text at 786.790.9217 and include your name and what you were treated for. We will tell you what is normal, what is not, and what to do next.

If you believe you are experiencing a medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department first.
